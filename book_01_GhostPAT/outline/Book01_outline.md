Prologue — “The Key That Should Have Been Dead”
	•	A rotating cursor blinking in a dark terminal window.
	•	You staring at logs, migraine, exhausted.
	•	Build deploy failing.
	•	A revoked token still authenticating.
	•	You realize StegVerse is either about to collapse — or evolve.

The Ghost_PAT breathes once, and the story begins.
